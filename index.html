What is so special?

  • Foam midsole offers lightweight, responsive cushioning.
  • Low-cut, padded collar looks sleek and feels comfortable.
  • Rubber outsole with classic hoops pivot circle adds durability, traction and heritage style.

What is the story?

Born from a series of mash-ups, hacks and tight deadlines, the Nike Dunk landed on college basketball courts during the '85–'86 season.

While the original 'College Colours' designs helped fans stay true to their school, the Dunk didn't prove to be popular.

But it was this humble sneaker's lack of popularity—and flat, grippy soles—that helped it jump the rails to captivate skateboarders.

Decades later, this everyday favourite continues to be snatched up in countless colourways, proving its influence is undeniable.
